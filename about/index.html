JAE SVN is an Argentine singer, rapper, songwriter, and music producer. He first started learning guitar and writing songs at the age of 8. He was always passionate about music, and he quickly developed his abilities for songwriting and guitar playing. In his teenage years, he took an interest in hip-hop and music production. He began to experiment with different styles of music, and he soon developed his own unique sound. As a result of his involvement with music, he created the HEPTAGON project in 2020. Two years later, Jae released his two first songs, "Discord" and "1000 Miles". In 2023, he only released his third single called "Shine", which could be considered the end of a trilogy following his last two songs. Starting 2024 he decided to end with the HEPTAGON project and take a different approach with his music, as a result, the name of the project was changed to KICK HAT SNARE.
